## Recipes

### Steamed white rice

* Ingredients:
  * **1 glass of rice**
  * **2 glasses of water**
  * **1 steam-cooking case**


* Process: 

> Mix homogenously and microwave in the steam-cooking case for 11 minutes. Add the mixture of sugar, salt and vinegar afterwards.
